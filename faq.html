Frequently Asked Questions

How can we help you?

Sizing & Materials

All our shirts are made from high-quality, 100% heavyweight cotton, sourced locally in the Philippines. They are designed for comfort and durability, providing a soft, structured feel that lasts.

Our clothing is unisex and designed for a relaxed, slightly oversized fit. We use a general unisex sizing guide. Please click the 'Size Guide' link on any product page to see the full sizing table.

Shipping & Return

We offer standard shipping nationwide (3-7 business days) for a flat rate of ₱50.00. Orders above ₱2,500.00 qualify for free shipping.

If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 14 days of receipt, provided it is unworn, unwashed, and in its original condition with all tags attached. Please contact our support team to initiate a return.

Payment & Orders

We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), GCash, Maya, and bank transfers. Cash on Delivery (COD) is available for select areas in Metro Manila.

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with your tracking number and a link to the courier's website. You can also find your tracking information in the "Pending Orders" section of your account page.
